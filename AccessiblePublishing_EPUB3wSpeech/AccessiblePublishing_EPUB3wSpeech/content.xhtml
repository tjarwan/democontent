ReadSpeaker is the worldwide leader in speech-enabling online content. In 1999, ReadSpeaker pioneered the first-ever speech-enabling application for websites and the first server-based DAISY production system “DAISY Generator” in 2003.

Since then, we have accumulated knowledge and experience in handling and converting many types of document formats.

We provide a portfolio of tools and services that cover talking digital books, structuring of PDFs, online document reading with support for many document formats, and customizable workflow solutions.

ReadSpeaker has a wide variety of more than 5000 publishing, education, corporate, media, government and not-for-profit customers worldwide subscribing to its services. ReadSpeaker is a member of the International Digital Publishing Forum (IDPF).

Accessible Publishing Solutions

ReadSpeaker Narrator

The solution for automatically creating your talking books.

We automatically and seamlessly create audio books for your blind, visually impaired, and dyslexic readers using high-quality, natural-sounding text to speech in 32 languages and in the following formats

  • DAISY (audio full text, audio only, audio + NCC)
  • EPUB 3 (using Media Overlays specification or byadding embedded audio files)
  • MP3

We support a variety of different input formats including:

  • DAISY Text
  • DTBook
  • EPUB
  • HTML
  • HTML5
  • HTML saved from MS Word
  • Custom XML
  • PDF

Document Structuring Services

The solution for adding structure to your PDF documents.

Our document structuring services are ideal for helping you convert unstructured PDFs into structured documents by using proper tagging. We provide automatic and semi-automatic structuring for your unstructured documents.

Online Document Reader

The solution to make your documents accessible to more readers by enabling them to listen on any connected device.

Our web-based online document reader supports a variety of different document formats, such as EPUB, DOC, DOCX, PDF, and EPUB 3. A unique feature is that it can both preserve the original layout and show the pages in HTML format allowing the end-user to customize how the content is being presented

The Document Reader can read text content in 24 languages using built-in text to speech. Fully web-based, it can be used from any device with a web browser. It also supports playing DAISY books as well as EPUB 3 with Media Overlay including full keyboard navigation.
