As a first approximation, a concept involves the linked network of a CL
and the various CMs it is linked to (the shaded parts of the figure and
the links between them). The inferential power associated with concepts
is derived in large measure from its CMs and the way they are embedded
in models. But the CL gives the concept an independence from particular
models that allows for application of multiple models, and for cross-model
reasoning.
