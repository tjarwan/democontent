primarily to an audience of theoretical cognitive scientists and attempts to
show that the specific features afforded by graphical models can be used

intentionally left open the questions of how the hypothesis of a pluralistic
model-based architecture for understanding might be developed (perhaps
in a variety of different ways for different domains) at Marr’s algorithmic
and implementational levels. Further exploration of my view within theo-
retical cognitive science would require more concrete hypotheses about the
types of models involved, and it is compatible with my views that some or
all of the modeling might turn out to be accommodated within some more
specific framework such as graphical modeling.
