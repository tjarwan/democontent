At first glance, the frame problem might not seem to have much to do
with Hume’s problem or the binding problem. It is generally presented as
a problem not about the constitution of objects but about which frame or
model (any one of which has its own internal ontology for thinking about
objects) to apply. Yet there is an important connection: we do not merely
use one model or another to think about an object, an event, a state of affairs,
or a situation. We often use several, applying them to the same object or
situation. And to do that, we need some way of coordinating them as apply-
ing to one and the same object or situation (and a way of individuating
“situations” as well as objects that is model independent) in a fashion that
does not depend wholly on any one model’s way of framing objects and
situations.
Relation to Triangulation
Each of these problems is about how we integrate different types of repre-
sentations into representations of a single thing and coordinate the types
of information and understanding afforded by different models and per-
ceptual systems. It may be that some of the sensory information falling
within the scope of Hume’s problem and the binding problem is not based
in models. If that is so, then there are issues about triangulation that are
not about triangulation through multiple models. Alternatively, if we do end
up having reason to regard even Humean concepts and early perceptual
representations as embedded in models, the problem is more generally a
problem of triangulation through models.
Cases
The ability to coordinate information derived from distinct perceptual
areas and mental models is one of the chief arguments that has been given
for the need of “central processing,” either in language or in a language-like
mental medium. To be sure, we do think in language and have language-
like ways of thinking that may be distinct from the use of a public language
in thinking. But this is not the only way of accomplishing such tasks and
indeed is not a plausible hypothesis about how it is achieved in some of the
cases. There do seem to be several distinct kinds of cases. I will survey those
I am aware of here, noting proposals for explaining some of them.
